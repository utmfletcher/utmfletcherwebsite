A Brief Overview

I’m the kind of person that jumps right into a task and tackles it head on. I learnt skiing that while it can be intimidating to lean forward into a steep slope with twists and turns it’s the only way to make it. This reflects my personality as I approach my goals and tasks in the same way, leaning into the uncertainty and unknown with dogged persistence.

I went to 6 different school between grades 1 and 6. As a result I make friends easily and include people that others might overlook. I know what its like to be bullied or excluded so I make every effort to include as many people as I can in my activities and groups.
